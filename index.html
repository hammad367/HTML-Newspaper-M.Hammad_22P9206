Death of Fatima Jinnah

Great Woman of a Great Nation

Wave of grief and anger across the country. The nation mourns the loss of Mother of the Nation.

Pakistan Deprived of a Great Leader and Light with the Death of Miss Fatima Jinnah

President Ayub Khan expressed deep sorrow and grief over the death of Miss Fatima Jinnah. The President said that the nation has lost a great leader who served the country with dedication. The funeral procession will depart from her residence at 2 PM, and burial will take place in the afternoon. Dignitaries from across the country are arriving to pay their final respects. The nation observes a day of mourning.

LAST MESSAGE

Miss Fatima Jinnah's last message to the nation was one of unity and perseverance. She urged the people to remain united and work for the progress of Pakistan. Her final words emphasized the importance of democracy and the rule of law. She will always be remembered as the Mother of the Nation who stood by her brother Muhammad Ali Jinnah in the struggle for Pakistan.

FUNERAL DETAILS

The funeral procession will depart from her residence at 2 PM. The burial ceremony will take place at the mausoleum premises. Thousands of mourners are expected to pay their final respects. Security arrangements have been made along the entire route. The entire nation observes a day of mourning. Government buildings are draped in black flags. Citizens from all walks of life gather to pay their respects to the departed soul of the Mother of the Nation.

The funeral arrangements have been completed with utmost care and respect. Security has been tightened around the route. Thousands of people are expected to attend the funeral prayers. Government officials, political leaders, and foreign dignitaries will be present to pay their final respects.

The nation has lost a great leader and visionary. Miss Fatima Jinnah's contributions to the independence movement and nation-building will be remembered for generations. Her dedication to democratic principles and social welfare set an example for all. The void left by her passing will be deeply felt across the country.

World leaders and dignitaries have expressed their deepest condolences. The international community mourns the loss of a great statesman and visionary. Her contributions to nation-building will be remembered for generations to come. The entire nation observes a day of mourning. Government buildings are draped in black flags. Citizens from all walks of life gather to pay their respects to the departed soul of the Mother of the Nation.

Political leaders from all parties have expressed their condolences.

Special prayers will be offered in mosques across the country for the departed soul.
